DRAGON BALL: Sparking! ZERO

DRAGON BALL: Sparking! ZERO is a 2024 arena fighter developed by Spike Chunsoft and published by Bandai Namco.

Master sophisticated combat systems as one of 182 characters from the Dragon Ball series, written by Akira Toriyama (R.I.P).


Release date: October 8th 2024
