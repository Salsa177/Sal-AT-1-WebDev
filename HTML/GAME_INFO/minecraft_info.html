Minecraft

Minecraft is a procedurally generated voxel-sandbox developed by Mojang Studios

Mine, craft and build in an infinitely-sized world, doing whatever your mind pleases, whether that be building a house, mining for diamonds or defeating the Ender Dragon.


Release date: November 18th 2011
Platforms:

  • Windows
  • Playstation
  • Xbox
  • Nintendo Switch
  • Mac OS
  • IOS/Android
  • Linux
