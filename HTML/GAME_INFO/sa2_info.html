Sonic Adventure 2

Sonic Adventure 2 is an action platformer published by SEGA

Follow Sonic, Shadow and friends as you play through various levels, uncovering the mysteries of Shadow's origin and prevent the destruction of the World.


Release date: June 18th 2001
Platforms:

  • Nintendo Gamecube
  • Sega Dreamcast
  • Windows
  • Xbox
  • Playstation
